💡 After saving, Pardot and Apollo enrichment may automatically populate additional fields (industry, company size, etc.) within 24 hours.
03
Edit / Update a Contact Field
⏱ ~1 minute📍 From the contact record
1
Open the contact record
Use Global Search (Guide 01) to find and open the contact you want to update.
2
Click the "Edit" button — or click directly on a field
You have two options: click the blue Edit button at the top to open the full edit form, or hover over any individual field and click the small pencil icon that appears to edit just that one field inline.

3
Make your changes
Update the field values. For text fields, just type. For picklists (dropdowns), click to see the options and select one. For lookups (like Account Name), start typing and select from the suggestions.

Click Save
Click the blue Save button to commit your changes. The record refreshes and shows the updated values. If you made a mistake, click Cancel to discard and start over.
⚠️ Do not navigate away before saving — your changes will be lost.

05
Check Campaign Member Statuses
⏱ ~1 minute📍 From the campaign record
1
Open the Campaign record
Search for the event by name using Global Search (e.g. "CCS 2026"). Click the campaign from the dropdown to open it.
2
Go to the Campaign Members tab
Click the Campaign Members tab in the record's tab bar (same as Guide 04, Step 2).
3
Read the Status column
Each row shows a member's name, company, and current status. Statuses are color-coded so you can scan quickly.

4
Update a status (if needed)
Click on any person's name to open their campaign member record. Click the Status field and choose a new value, then Save. Or, to update many people at once, ask Aaron — bulk status updates are done via data import.
💡 Need to see statuses for a specific person across all events? Use the Event Registration Lookup tool instead — it shows all 2026 event registrations for any contact in one view.
